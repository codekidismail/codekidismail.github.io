About Myself

Welcome to my website! My name is Ismail Hamza and I am a student at Al Yasmin International School in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. I am honored to be participating in the Digital Fest competition with my project, "Parent's Point-Based Monitoring Tool for their Children." Phase 1 of this project is now complete, and in Phase 2 I will be adding even more features and enhancements, such as integration with a SQL database, as I continue to learn new technologies.

This innovative website is designed to help parents encourage their children to adopt healthy habits by creating profiles for their children, inputting information about their food plan and activities, and using a meal planner to ensure that their children are getting the necessary nutrients. The website tracks the points earned by each child and displays the results at the end of each day, week, and month. To further motivate their children to continue practicing healthy habits, parents can also offer rewards to children who earn the most points in a month.

In the future, I plan to add even more features to the website, such as monthly reports, a rewards system, bonus points for the following month, nutritional information, educational resources, and even a mobile application. I am grateful for the support and guidance of my parents as I have been learning about HTML, CSS, JS, and SQL. I am excited to pursue a career in the field of AI and ML and I appreciate your support in helping me get selected and win the Digital Fest competition by linking and sharing my video with your friends and family. Thank you for learning about my project and for supporting kids in learning the importance of healthy habits.
